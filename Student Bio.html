Student Bio

Gail was admitted to the July 2017 UCBerkeley Extension Coding Bootcamp. For 24 weeks Gail will learn about the tools, practice the skills and build a portfolio of front-end and back-end software development. The full stack knowledge and experience will allow Gail to grow in her current industry and/or expand into other industries. She looks forward to the opportunities!

Professional Bio

Gail is a beloved bartender in San Francisco. She has been at the forefront of many cocktail changing movements/revolutions working to provide better quality products, perpetuating knowledge and creating a welcome environment for all.

As a lifelong learner, Gail has challenged herself in other areas while continuing the pursuit of education. She worked at UCSF's Huang Lab as well as an in-house counsel firm for Liberty Mutual Insurance.

Personal Statement

Gail has lived in many different places but for the last 15 years, she has made San Francisco her home. Scuba diving is one of her favorite hobbies. She has attained the PADI Rescue Diver level. Also, she enjoys practicing yoga, riding her bicycle, dabbling in languages and spending time ruminating. Because of her diverse background, Gail is open-minded and happy to meet new people.
